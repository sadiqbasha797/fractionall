The Fraction Car Story

At Fraction Car, we're on a mission to redefine car ownership in India. We believe that owning a luxury car should be accessible to more people, without the traditional financial burdens. Our platform enables co-ownership of premium vehicles, making the dream of driving a high-end car a reality for many. Fraction Car stands out by offering a unique fractional ownership model. Instead of bearing the full cost of a car, you share it with a group of co-owners. This significantly reduces your financial commitment, eliminating the need for large loans or EMIs. We handle all the management aspects, from maintenance to insurance, ensuring a hassle-free experience. Our vision is to create a community of car enthusiasts who can enjoy the thrill of driving luxury vehicles without the usual ownership challenges. We aim to expand our platform, offering a wider range of cars and services, and making fractional car ownership the preferred choice for discerning drivers across India.
